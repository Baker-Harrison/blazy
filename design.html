A dark, flat interface for a terminal app

Every color, type rule, and component below is taken directly from the design tokens in src/renderer/index.css. No shadows, no gradients, no borders — the titlebar is flush with the app background, distinguished only by the button group.

Titlebar component

Blazy

Hover the buttons above — try min, max, and close. Titlebar background is flush with the app background (no label, no border) — the whole bar is a drag handle (-webkit-app-region: drag) except the button group, which opts back out (no-drag).

Sidebar component

Blazy

Same flat background as the app and titlebar — no border, no separate surface tone. The app name lives here now, centered at the top, instead of in the content area.
